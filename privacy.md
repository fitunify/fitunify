  # Privacy Policy
                                                                                                                                        
  **FitUnify** respects your privacy.
                                                                                                                                        
  ## Data Collection                                        
  FitUnify stores all personal data (weight, nutrition logs, workout history)
  locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.                                                                             
  No personal data is collected or stored by the app developer.                                                                         
                                                                                                                                        
  ## Third-Party Services                                                                                                               
  - **Fitbit API**: Used to sync steps and weight data from your Fitbit account                                                         
    with your explicit authorization via OAuth 2.0.         
  - **AI Providers (Gemini / Claude)**: Coaching queries include anonymized fitness                                                     
    context. Review Google's and Anthropic's privacy policies for how they                                                              
    handle API data.                                                                                                                    
  - **USDA / Open Food Facts**: Food search queries are sent to these public APIs.                                                      
                                                                                                                                        
  ## Your Rights                                            
  You can delete all local data at any time by deleting the app.
